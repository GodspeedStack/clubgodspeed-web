# Godspeed Basketball - Design System

## Single Source of Truth

Version: 1.2 (Strict Blue Update) | Status: Active

1. Brand Foundation
Mission: Building complete players through science and confidence.

Visual DNA: High-contrast, Athletic, Royal Blue Accents.

1. Color System
Primary Palette
Black (#000000): Authority, Backgrounds, Primary Text.

Brand Blue (#2563eb): Primary Buttons, Highlights, Links, Logo Accent.

- HEX: #2563eb
- RGB: rgb(37, 99, 235)
- HSL: hsl(221, 83%, 53%)

Interaction Blue (#1d4ed8): Hover states for buttons.

- HEX: #1d4ed8
- RGB: rgb(29, 78, 216)
- HSL: hsl(224, 76%, 48%)

White (#FFFFFF): Text on dark backgrounds.

Red (#EF4444): Notifications / Alerts only.

1. Typography
Font Family: 'Inter', sans-serif.

Hero Title: 3.5rem (56px) - 5rem (80px), Black (900), Uppercase.

Section Title: 2.5rem (40px), Black (900), Uppercase.

1. Components
Buttons
Primary: Brand Blue Background (#2563eb), White Text, Uppercase, Pill Shape (rounded-full).
Hover: Interaction Blue (#1d4ed8).

Secondary: White/Transparent Background, Blue Border (2px) OR White Background (on dark), Pill Shape.

Navigation
Desktop: White background, Black text.

Active State: Text turns Blue (#2563eb) on hover.

1. Do's and Don'ts
✅ DO: Use Blue (#2563eb) for all primary actions.

❌ DON'T: Use Gold, Green, or Cyan.

❌ DON'T: Use square buttons (Always Pill/Rounded).
