# AcademyOS Design System

## Typography

- **Primary Font:** 'Inter', sans-serif
- **Weights:** 400 (Body), 700 (Bold), 900 (Black/Headers)

## Buttons

- **Primary:** `px-8 py-4 bg-blue-600 text-white font-bold rounded-full`
- **Secondary (Light):** `border-2 border-gray-100 text-black hover:border-gray-300`
- **Secondary (Dark):** `bg-white text-black hover:bg-gray-200`

## Container Widths

- **Max Width:** `max-w-7xl mx-auto`
- **Padding:** `px-6 md:px-20`
