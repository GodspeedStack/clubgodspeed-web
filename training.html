COMMON QUESTIONS

What is the time commitment?

We expect players to train at least 2x per week. Consistency is the only way to see real results.

Do you travel for tournaments?

Yes, we will begin to travel for major tournaments. We believe in exposing players to high-level competition outside their local bubble.

Is this for beginners?

Our programs are designed for serious athletes. While we welcome motivated beginners, the intensity is high. We recommend a trial session to see if it's a good fit.
