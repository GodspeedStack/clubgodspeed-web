Meet the Coaches

Coach Scott

Head Coach & Founder

Godspeed Basketball was founded to develop young men through a blend of movement science, basketball intelligence, and identity.

Coach Scott's coaching bridges biomechanics, cognitive skill-building, and identity-rooted in purpose. The goal is to create an environment where players learn to move efficiently, think clearly, and lead boldly.

Coach True

Player Development

Coach True brings intensity and detail to player development. His focus on fundamental mastery and game-speed application ensures players are ready for any level of competition.

Coach Gene

Community & Mentorship

Coach Gene is a veteran coach and dedicated educator with decades of experience developing athletes both on and off the court. He bridges the gap between the game and life, bringing a wealth of wisdom to mentorship and ensuring every player feels part of the Godspeed family and succeeds beyond the court.

Community Leader

Coaching Style

Direct & Demanding

We build lasting confidence through accountability. Supportive but firm. No shortcuts. We prioritize precision first, speed second. By holding our athletes to a standard that demands their best effort, we help them discover what they are truly capable of.

Holistic Approach

Players are coached as complete people. We focus on teaching emotional regulation and empowering each athlete to uplift one another as brothers, fostering a true family atmosphere where no one is left behind.

Training Philosophy

Movement Science

Principles for efficiency, speed, and injury prevention tailored to basketball movements.

Modern IQ

Decision-making training for the modern, positionless game ensuring high adaptability.

Psychology

Confidence building techniques derived from global performance coaches.

Identity

Purpose-driven mentorship founded on strong character and personal responsibility.
