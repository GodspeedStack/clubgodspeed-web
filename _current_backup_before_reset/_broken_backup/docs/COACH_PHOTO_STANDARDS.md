# Godspeed Coach Portrait Standard

## 1. Lighting Setup (The "Butterfly" Technique)

The lighting is the most critical part of this image. It uses a setup often called **Butterfly Lighting** or **Paramount Lighting**, commonly used for athletes and dramatic portraits to emphasize facial structure.

* **Main Light (Key Light):** Single hard light source placed high and directly in front (butterfly shadow under nose) and slightly above eye level.
* **Highlight Placement:** Strong sheen on the top center of the forehead, bridge of nose, and the apples of the cheeks.
* **Falloff:** Rapid falloff towards ears/neck for a slimming effect; sides of the face in shadow.
* **Catchlights:** Tiny, sharp white reflections in upper center of pupils.

## 2. Camera & Lens Settings

* **Focal Length:** 85mm or 105mm (portrait length, no distortion).
* **Aperture:** f/4 to f/5.6 (face sharp, shoulders softening slightly).
* **Angle:** Eye level.

## 3. Subject & Pose

* **Positioning:** Squared directly to camera (shoulders even).
* **Expression:** Genuine, confident "Duchenne" smile (visible teeth, crinkled eyes - warm but intense).
* **Grooming:** Well-groomed stubble; shaved head should catch the "halo" light on the crown.

## 4. Wardrobe & Styling

* **Garment:** Black athletic quarter-zip pullover (Nike).
* **Details:** Zipper pulled to collarbone (not fully zipped).
* **Branding:** White Nike Swoosh on viewer's left chest. Must appear to "float" against the black background.

## 5. Background & Environment

* **Backdrop:** Pure Black (`#000000`).
* **Technique:** "Figure-ground merging" (black shirt blends seamlessly into the black background). **No rim light.**

## 6. Post-Processing

* **Conversion:** High-contrast Black & White.
* **Tone:** "Crushed" blacks (deepened until detail is lost in shadows), boosted highlights (skin reflection, teeth, eyes, logo) to pure white.
* **Texture:** Increased Clarity/Structure for a gritty, athletic feel.
* **Vignette:** Darkened corners to lock focus on the face.

## Visual Reference (The "Recipe")

**DO NOT DEVIATE** from this look for any Godspeed Coach.

* **Gear:** Camera with 85mm lens.
* **Backdrop:** Black paper/cloth.
* **Light:** One strobe with a Beauty Dish (for crisp contrast) or small softbox, placed 2 feet above subject's head, angled down at 45 degrees.
* **Edit:** Turn to B&W, increase Contrast +30, increase Clarity +15, drag "Blacks" slider down until shirt merges with background.
